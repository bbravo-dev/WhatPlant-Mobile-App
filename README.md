## 📱 Android APK (Demo)

You can download and test the Android version here:

👉 [Download APK](https://github.com/bbravo-dev/WhatPlant-Mobile-App/releases/latest)

WhatPlant is a mobile app that helps you manage and care for your plants. You can scan plants using your camera or select images from your gallery to identify them. The app allows you to add reminders for watering and other care tasks, and helps you keep track of your plants. It can also access your location to provide guidance on whether it is suitable to keep your plants outdoors based on local conditions.
